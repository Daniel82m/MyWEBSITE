Robert Lewandowski

Robert Lewandowski is a Polish professional footballer.
As a striker he plays for Bundesliga club Bayern Munich

He is the captain of the Poland national team.
He has scored over 600 senior career goals for club and country.
Lewandowski is considered one of the best strikers of all time

FIFA Men's Player, and UEFA Men's Player of the Year in 2019-2020.
Ballon d'Or Striker of the Year, European Golden Shoe, and breaking records in 2020
